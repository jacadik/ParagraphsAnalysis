About Document Comparison

The document comparison tool allows you to:

  • Compare two documents to find similarities and differences
  • Identify matching paragraphs across documents
  • Get a similarity score between documents
  • Highlight differences in text content

This is particularly useful for template analysis to identify common structures and variable parts.
